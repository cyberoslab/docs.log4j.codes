DASHBOARD HELP
BY MOJIO

Welcome to MOJIO Dashboard.

  • MOJIO dashboard is customizable and you can add your Gears to dashboard.
  • Each Gear designed to show different data.
  • To add new Gear use     icon in top right corner. It open a list of all gears and select your favorite ones.
  • To remove any gear use     icon in top left corner of gear. It open a contect menu. the first option is remove gear.
  • To change gears order use     icon in top left corner of gear. It open a contect menu. there is two option to move gear up or down.
  • If you have multiple vehicle you can switch the vehicle by clicking on vehicle name in top left corner of dashboard.
